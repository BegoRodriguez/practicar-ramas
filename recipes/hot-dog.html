Hot Dog

Description

This hot dog is a Windy City classic and a big favorite with sports fans!

Whatever you do, don't spoil the splendor of this Chicago dog with ketchup!

Ingredients

  • 1 all-beef hot dog
  • 1 poppy seed hot dog bun
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon sweet green picke relish
